Farmers Market

1. Healthy, Equitable Food Access & Education

  • Increase support for healthy, local food programs:
    • School meals (before, during, after school, summer)
    • EBT (Electronic Benefit Transfer)
    • Senior Farmers Market Vouchers
    • WIC (Women, Infants, and Children)
    • Senior dining services
    • Food banks and pantries
  • Expand affordable, healthy food access through local farming

2. Food Recovery & Environment

  • Reduce waste and increase food/agriculture/packaging recovery
  • Support sustainable agriculture and biodiversity-friendly practices

3. Local Food Economy

  • Support businesses selling healthy, local food, especially BIPOC-owned
  • Advocate for a healthy, safe, and fairly paid food system workforce

4. Hyperlocal Action & Resident Engagement

  • Strengthen collective action at the neighborhood level
  • Elevate resident leadership in food system decisions

5. Policy & Advocacy

  • Work with stakeholders to support local food access policies
  • Develop and advocate city, county, state, and federal policies
